Staying awake

This website will talk about staying awake at night.

Introduction

Staying awake simply means to not sleep when we should. Some might ask:"Why stay awake then?" However, there are good reasons and methods of doing it.

Why should we stay awake at night?

There are many reasons why people should stay awake. Among those reasons, the reason for most of the people including me will probably because there is work left to do. If we stay awake, we can finish the work we have left.

How to stay awake

Staying awake can be easy with just a cup of coffee and your strong mindset. These are the steps of how to stay awake at night:

  1. Stop doing what you are doing now if you feel sleepy.
  2. Drink coffee - caffeine in the coffee will help you.
  3. Quickly wash your face or take a shower - it will be refreshing.
  4. Think that you will finish your work as quickly as possible and go to sleep - this will make you determined to do your work.
  5. Start doing what you have to do.

You can check if you are following these steps correctly by:

Constantly checking the clock! If you find yourself being awake every hour without falling asleep, you have succeeded. It's even better to go to bed after. If you fell asleep without noticing it and find yourself awake hours later, you have failed.

Conclusion

Congratulations, you have mastered the ability to stay awake! Practice them so you can slowly build your skills!
